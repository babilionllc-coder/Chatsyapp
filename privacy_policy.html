Privacy Policy for ChatSY

Last updated: September 27, 2024

Quick Summary: ChatSY is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect minimal data necessary for app functionality and AI processing. Your conversations are processed by third-party AI services (OpenAI, DeepSeek, Google Gemini) but are not stored permanently on our servers.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Data You Provide Directly

  • Chat Messages: Text you input for AI processing
  • Account Information: Email address (if you choose to register)
  • Device Information: Device ID and type for app functionality
  • Usage Data: App features you use and preferences

1.2 Data Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system, app version
  • Usage Analytics: Feature usage, app crashes, performance data
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type (for web version)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • AI Processing: Send your messages to AI providers (OpenAI, DeepSeek, Google Gemini) for response generation
  • App Functionality: Provide chat features, document analysis, voice synthesis
  • Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve app performance
  • Support: Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support

3. Third-Party AI Services

ChatSY uses the following AI services to provide responses:

  • OpenAI (GPT-5): OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • DeepSeek: DeepSeek Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini: Google Privacy Policy
  • ElevenLabs: ElevenLabs Privacy Policy
  • Tavily Search: Tavily Privacy Policy

Important: When you use ChatSY, your messages are sent to these third-party services for processing. Please review their privacy policies to understand how they handle your data.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • AI Service Providers: Your messages are sent to AI providers for processing
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Service Providers: With trusted third parties who assist in app operations

5. Data Security

  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • API keys are securely stored and not exposed to users
  • We implement industry-standard security measures
  • Regular security audits and updates

6. Data Retention

  • Chat Messages: Not permanently stored on our servers
  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized, retained for up to 2 years
  • Support Data: Retained for up to 3 years for customer service purposes

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Export your data in a common format
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics and non-essential data collection

8. Children's Privacy

ChatSY is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own, including the United States where our AI service providers are located. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Sending you an email notification (if you have an account)
